Story
You are a student walking home at night. On the way, you see a mysterious cave.

Decision 1

Input:
Do you enter the cave?
1. Yes
2. No

If answer == 1
    Go inside the cave.
Else
    Go home safely.
    END

Decision 2 (Inside the cave)

Input:
You find two paths.
1. Left
2. Right

If answer == 1
    You find a treasure chest.
Else
    You meet a monster.

Decision 3 (Treasure)

Input:
Open the chest?
1. Yes
2. No

If answer == 1
    You receive 100 gold.
    GOOD ENDING
Else
    You leave the cave safely.
    NORMAL ENDING

Decision 4 (Monster)

Input:
Fight or Run?
1. Fight
2. Run

If answer == 1
    The monster defeats you.
    BAD ENDING
Else
    You escape safely.
    NORMAL ENDING
